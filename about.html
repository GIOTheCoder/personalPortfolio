Carlos Barnes is a junior developer with the MS Coding Academy (MSCA). He is also a graphic artist with 9+ years of experience. After graduating from the MSCA, Carlos plans to use his graphic design experience and web development skills to create beautiful, user-friendly web applications. Before attending the MSCA, Carlos attended Jackson State University. After about two years, he decided to start operating his own freelance graphic design business full time. With a client retention rate of over 94%, he has worked with many different companies including Def Jam record label, My Brother’s Keeper non-profit organization, and Southwire LLC. Carlos’s passion for digital media and eagerness to learn drove him to enroll in the MSCA. He plans to pursue a career where he will be able to work with a software team and continue his education as a full stack developer. In his freetime, Carlos enjoys spending time with his family and playing video games. He also enjoys watching and listening to different podcasts.
